The Witch World stories
in order of publication

This is adapted from the “Reading Order” page at Elwher’s Witch-World wiki (as edited 23 Aug 2010).

The first three novels were published in an omnibus edition, The Gates to Witch World (“GtWW”). The next three are included in another omnibus, Lost Lands of the Witch World (“LLoWW”). “AN” means Andre Norton.

  1. Witch World (1963; in GtWW)
  2. Web of the Witch World (1964; in GtWW)
  3. Year of the Unicorn (1965; in GtWW)
  4. Three Against the Witch World (1965; in LLoWW)
  5. Warlock of the Witch World (1967; in LLoWW)
  6. Sorceress of the Witch World (1968; in LLoWW)
  7. "Ully the Piper" (1970, in eg. High Sorcery, Ace 1970)
  8. The Crystal Gryphon (1972)
  9. Spell of the Witch World (3 stories, DAW 1972)
  10. “One Spell Wizard” (in Garan the Eternal, DAW 1972)
  11. “Legacy from Sorn Fen” (in Garan the Eternal, DAW 1972, and Lore...)
  12. “The Toads of Grimmerdale” (1973; in eg. Lore of the Witch World)
  13. The Jargoon Pard (1974)
  14. “Spider Silk” (1976; in Lore of the Witch World)
  15. Trey of Swords (1977)
  16. “Sword of Unbelief” (1977; in Lore of the Witch World)
  17. Zarsthor’s Bane (1978)
  18. “Sand Sister” (1979; in Lore of the Witch World)
  19. “Falcon Blood” (1979; in Lore of the Witch World)
  20. “Changeling” (1980; in Lore of the Witch World)
  21. Lore of the Witch World (7 stories; DAW 1980)
  22. Gryphon in Glory (1981)
  23. Horn Crown (1981)
  24. ’Ware Hawk (1983)
  25. Gryphon’s Eyrie (1984)
  26. “Were-Wrath” (1984; associational; in Wizards’ Worlds, Tor 1989)
  27. The Gate of the Cat (1987)
  28. Tales of the Witch World (17 stories, 1 by AN; Tor, 1987)
  29. Tales of the Witch World 2 (17 stories, none by AN; Tor, 1988)
  30. Four from the Witch World (4 stories, none by AN; Tor, 1989)
  31. Tales of the Witch World 3 (19 stories, none by AN; Tor, 1990)
  32. Storms of Victory (2 novels, 1991; vol. 1 of WW: The Turning)
  33. Flight of Vengeance (2 novels, 1992; vol. 2 of WW: The Turning)
  34. Songsmith (1992; with A.C. Crispin)
  35. On Wings of Magic (2 novels, 1994; vol. 3 of WW: The Turning)
  36. The Key of the Keplian (1995; with Lyn McConchie; vol. 1 of Secrets of the WW)
  37. The Magestone (1996; with Mary H. Schaub; vol. 2 of Secrets of the WW)
  38. The Warding of the Witch World (1996; by AN alone; vol. 3 of Secrets of the WW)
  39. Ciara’s Song (1998; with Lyn McConchie)
  40. “Earthborne” (in Masters of Fantasy, Baen 2004)
  41. The Duke’s Ballad (2005; with Lyn McConchie; sequel to Ciara’s Song)
  42. Silver May Tarnish (2005; with Lyn McConchie)
