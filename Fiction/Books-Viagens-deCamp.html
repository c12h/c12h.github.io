Here is a list of the stories, taken from Wikipedia's article “Viagen Interplanetarias” as of 29 July 2015. I have not checked their dates.

  • Titles in “double quotes” are short stories, those in ‘single quotes’ are novellas and those in italics are novels.
  • All of these Baen books are No Longer For Sale (NLFS).
  • The Venom Trees of Sunga is only available as a paperback from Amazon US.
