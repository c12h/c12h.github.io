showing authors and collections

This is adapted from the “Reading Order” page at Elwher’s Witch-World wiki (as edited 23 Aug 2010).   I (CWC) added the author and collection info myself.   The right-hand column contains notes on the internal order of the stories from Elwher’s wiki, mostly intact.
