(BTW: Amazon Prime is AU$7 per month.)

Crunchyroll, Saturdays 01:00 AEDT Boogiepop and Others
Based on a popular light novel series; ANN guys like it
Wait a few weeks, then try early episodes.
Amazon Prime Dororo
Darkish historical fantasy (?); ANN reviewers like it
Maybe watch a few episodes just in case I like it?
Crunchyroll, Sundays 05:30 AEDT ENDRO!
Comedy about four silly but very moe girls at an Adventurer School.
Extremely moe (satire?), but has some laughs.
Crunchyroll, Fridays 03:30 AEDT Girly Air Force
SF adventure (mysterious flyers devastating humanity) with good worldbuilding
Surprisingly good (tho not great) so far.
Crunchyroll, Sundays 3:00 AEDT Kaguya-sama: Love is War
A comedy-romance about two elite high-schoolers trying to force each other to confess first.
ANN guys fairly positive.
Has enough laughs
Amazon Prime Kemurikusa
Another post-apocalyptic SF series from TATSUKI, who did Kemono Friends
Worth a look, going by the ANN reviews.
AnimeLab Magical Girl Spec-Ops Asuka
A dark magical girl show: magical girls are child soldiers, resulting in PTSD and other psychological problems.
Rated 3.5 by Rebecca, 4.5 by Theron.
Maybe worth a look?
Crunchyroll, Tuesdays 03:30 AEDT Mob Psycho 100 II
Watch both series.
Crunchyroll, Sundays 00:30 AEDT The Morose Mononokean II
James Beckett says it’s a “gentle and moderately entertaining comedy”.
Try S1 first.
Crunchyroll, Thursdays 04:00 AEDT My Roommate is a Cat
ANN folks are fairly neutral
Will watch, if only for the recaps from the cat's POV.
Crunchyroll, Saturdays 03:00 AEDT The Price of Smiles
“an ostensibly-cutesy-but-actually-super-serious sci-fi mecha battle war drama romance. I think.” — James Beckett
Wait a few weeks, then try early episodes.
Crunchyroll, Thursdays 02:00 AEDT The Promised Neverland
Shonen horror about an orphange
Maybe worth a look?
Crunchyroll, Fridays 09:00 AEDT The Quintessential Quintuplets
High-school boy gets tutoring work with 5 female classmates. Comedy/Slice-of-life/Romance.
One ANN guy says it’s funny.
Will watch: good character-driven(!) humor.
HIDIVE Real Girl season 2
ANN guys both had mixed feelings
Try S1 first.
Crunchyroll, Thursdays 02:30 AEDT The Rising of the Shield Hero
SJWs outraged.
Nice reworking of Isekai tropes; will watch.
